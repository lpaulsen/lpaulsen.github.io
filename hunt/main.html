YAPH

(Yet Another Puzzle Hunt)
by Luke Paulsen

Welcome to YAPH! This short puzzle hunt is currently open for test-solving.

The hunt consists of the pages linked below: eight puzzles (in no particular order) plus a meta-puzzle that depends on their answers. Nothing on this page is a puzzle.

This site will tell you whether answers are correct but it doesn't do any tracking, so you have unlimited time, team members, and answer attempts. (Please use this power responsibly.) All answers consist of normal English characters A-Z; capitalization and spacing are ignored. When you discover the correct answer to the meta-puzzle, you win!

If you need a hint on a puzzle, contact the organizer. The puzzles are untested, so don't be afraid to request hints when you get stuck.

Good luck and have fun!
